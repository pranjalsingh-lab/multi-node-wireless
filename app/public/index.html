How to read & tweak it

Who reads what

The pipeline runs one way. The Tilt Sensor reads the ADXL372 over SPI and advertises the raw X/Y/Z. The Lighting Hub is the only board that listens to the sensor - it maps the horizontal tilt to a brightness % and advertises that. The Smart Bulb listens only to the hub and drives its light to that intensity. Nobody opens a connection; every hop is a connectionless BLE beacon.

What you can change

  • Drag the Tilt sensor's X / Y / Z sliders - the orientation changes live.
  • Hit View code on any card to read the exact firmware source.
  • Compile your own image and Upload .elf to run it; Default reverts.

    What's happening here?
    
    
    This lab boots three virtual Nordic nRF52840 boards running real firmware on emulated hardware to form a one-way pipeline over a virtual Bluetooth LE link:
    
    
      
      
    • The Tilt Sensor reads its accelerometer and broadcasts orientation.
      • 
      
    • The Lighting Hub hears that, turns the tilt into a brightness, and broadcasts it.
      • 
      
    • The Smart Bulb hears the brightness and lights up to match.
